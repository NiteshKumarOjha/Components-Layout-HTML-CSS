01

How can I place an order online?

Yes, we have a minimum order requirement for online delivery. The minimum order amount may vary depending on your location and the delivery service we use. To find out the specific minimum order amount for your area, simply enter your delivery address on our online ordering platform, and it will be displayed during the checkout process.

  • Visit our online ordering platform.
  • Enter your delivery address.
  • Minimum order amount for your area will be shown during checkout.
